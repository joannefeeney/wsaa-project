ABOUT THE GROCER

The Grocer was founded in 2024 by Joanne Feeney. Originally hailing from County Sligo in Ireland, she moved to Dublin in 2021 and saw an opportunity to open an artisan grocer to provide the best products to the local area.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh baked pastries/cakes and other good stuff.

"I've always been a great lover of fresh coffee and even fresher produce."

Owner: Joanne Feeney

Opening hours: Every day from 8am to 5pm.

Address: 123 Main Street, Drimnagh, Co. Dublin
